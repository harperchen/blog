Science Writing

Posted on 2020-09-13 Edited on 2021-02-01 In Ph.D. Views:

  1. http://iacoma.cs.uiuc.edu/iacoma-papers/ssp19.pdf

Otherwises:

  1. https://nebelwelt.net/files/18CCS.pdf

Hints:

Technical writing require to be concise; better use common words and short sentences instead of “GRE-level vocabularies” and prolong sentences. The gist is that your technical content is already sort of difficult, then do not impose extra difficulty in reading.

Words:

practically virtually 几乎

essentially basically fundamentally 实际上 本质上

specially particularly 具体来说 专门

loosely roughly 大致上 差不多

typically 一般情况下

consequently 因此

conceptually 概念上
